FAQ accordion (native <details>)

What does "A2 desi cow milk" actually mean?

Indigenous Indian breeds (Gir, Sahiwal, Tharparkar) produce milk with only the A2 beta-casein protein. We only keep indigenous breeds.

How is bilona ghee different?

Milk → curd → churn → makhan → ghee. Most commercial ghee skips straight from cream to ghee via centrifuge. We do bilona.
