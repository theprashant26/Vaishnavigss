Our Sankalp

Cow welfare first.
Commerce, second.

Vaishnavi Gau Seva Gausansthan began with one Gir cow and a quiet promise: that she would never know hunger, fear, or neglect. Twelve years later, thirty-five indigenous cows share that promise. They are named, known, loved.

We sell what they give us — pure A2 milk, bilona ghee, fresh paneer — not because we are a business, but because they need fodder, shelter, and medicine. You buy. They thrive. The dharma continues.
